Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Can existing Vagrant boxes be used in Boxupp ?
Yes. Boxupp is a wrapper on top of Vagrant. With that said, any boxes which can be referred to by Vagrant installation on your computer can be used in Boxupp. They won't be downloaded again, if available locally.
Login credentials are stored locally ?
Yes, Login credentials are stored locally on your system and user authentication is handled there itself with no call beings directed outside your system which is running Boxupp
I am facing some issues using the tool. What to do ?
Please raise a ticket at https://github.com/BoxUpp/boxupp/issues or get in touch directly over mail at support@boxupp.com
Will you be adding support for more providers in future ?
Yes, we plan to add support for VMWare, XEN, AWS and more such providers in future.
